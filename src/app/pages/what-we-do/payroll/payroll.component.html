Payroll Process

Payroll processing is central to every business as it deals with employee payments, calculating salary and tax deductions and reporting everything in payroll tax filing.By outsourcing payroll processing service to experts will make this process incredibly easier for your businessas it reduces the time taken in keeping all the records. Our well-trained team of experts can help you with all aspects of payroll processing and thereby eliminate your need to train employees and invest in expensive payroll tools & software. As we need to deal with vast amount of employee data from many different clients, we are very serious when it comes to ensuring data security and keeping all your information secure.

Our payroll services include but are not limited to the following:

  • Payroll processing
  • Payroll entries and record keeping
  • Payroll reconciliation
  • Payroll tax filing and Year-end tax filing & reporting.

When you outsource payroll processing to our well qualified CA’s, we take care of all aspects such as creating payroll sheets, reports, paychecks, calculating taxes owed to federal and state agencies and deducting any employee insurance and benefits. We use the latest tools and expertise to make sure that your payroll is in order and there are no IRS penalties. The process will vary based on each organization depending on the structure, tax system & state rules and regulations. Our experts are trained to handle payroll processing for all types of businesses and providing detailed reports that will also give you a general idea of cost of each employee.

Payroll entries consist of employee record keeping and it not just involves pay slips and tax sheets but other useful information like attendance, vacation & sick days, bonuses, increments, new employees, transfers & promotions. The structure of this information will vary based on the type of organization. By taking in all relevant employee information will allow us to calculate the gross salary and adjusting for any deductions, social security & insurance benefits. We realize the importance of streamlining this process for your business by offering tailored services based on your organizational structure.

Payroll reconciliation is an important part of making sure that all data in payroll reports matches with payroll expense reports. We will reconcile everything in the payroll register including wages, deductions, hours worked and other entries. This step is necessary to ensure that all the work put in by the employees is accounted for and you avoid any chances of conflicts, penalties or even lawsuits.We have expertise to help you with payroll tax filing for state payroll taxes in the form of income taxes and unemployment taxes. We will make sure that the appropriate forms and guidelines are followed in preparing the wage reports for tax filing purposes.
