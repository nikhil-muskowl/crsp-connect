It is important for every business to reflect the human characteristics of giving back to the society. At CRSP, we care deeply about environmental and welfare issues. We aim to spread the message of nurturing our environment by promoting green lifestyle and eco friendly practices at the workplace. We constantly strive to eliminate wastage of resources and office supplies to promote a well-preserved environment.

We have also taken initiative to frequently engage ourselves in volunteering activities to address some of issues in our society and take steps to combat them.

We take pride in providing access to comprehensive training so that people who wish to work can become qualified. They should be able enough to handle basic accounting and administrative tasks to increase their suitability of contributing back to our organisation.

We frequently offer internship positions to motivated students and less experienced workers. The goal of this program is to educate workforce and make them ready for their professional careers. It also gives us an opportunity to bring in young talent and get new perspectives on our work culture. We include them in all our company discussions and give them enough knowledge and feedback that helps them realize their potential and choose their respective career paths.
