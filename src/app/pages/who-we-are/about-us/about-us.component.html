About Us

CRSP Connect LLC USA is wholly owned subsidiary of CRSP Connect Pvt. Limited, India providing Knowledge Process Outsourcing services to CPA firms and other businesses across the USA.

We specialize in quality bookkeeping, accountancy, payroll, taxation services, medical billing services, inventory management of various businesses as per the needs of our clients.

We have our corporate office in New York, USA and Indian office at Udaipur, Rajasthan, India.

CRSP Connect is managed by a team of qualified accountancy and financial professionals having more than 35 years of experience.

We feel that Outsourcing your finance and accounting tasks to CRSP Connect will encourage you to focus on your organizational growth as well as profitability. We are committed to provide special and personalized attention to our clients. This confidence of giving such commitment comes from years of practice, training and dedication.

Our staff is comprehensively trained and qualified in all technical aspects of accounting and taxation. They hold professional qualifications from accredited institutions and are extremely well versed with practical matters like local regulations and guidelines. We provide detailed training to our staff to communicate and understand client needs by providing seamless experience.

Our well-built infrastructure accommodates all our systems and staff by providing a productive working environment. We have established strict quality control measures to check accuracy and quality of each project at all phases of completion. Everyone at CRSP understands the importance of delivering results under strict deadlines and there is always a sense of unity among our team members to complete projects under the promised time frame.
