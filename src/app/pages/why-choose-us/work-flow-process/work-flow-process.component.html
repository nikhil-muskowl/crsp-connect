Work Flow Process

Our typical process for outsourcing work is described in the form of a flow chart below:

At the beginning, our experts will analyze your requirementsto drawup a suitable work plan. We will discuss the specifics of the plan in more detail and take your input to make improvements.

As per the work plan, CRSP will divert its resources to the work assigned. We will split the work based on difficulty so that complex work is handled by team of experts with 5-6 years of experience. And, regular work will be handled by

The completed work is reviewed by our Quality Assurance team and finally by our CPA before it is submitted for your review. The CPAwill be the single point contact between the Client and CRSP. We will share a monthly MIS with the client to keep track of the progress.

Queries to non-reconciled or pending data will be sent to client and regular follow up will be done through email or telecomm to resolve the issues.

Communication flow between CRSP and Sam Carter & Co.

Secure Communication Channel will be setup between two companies. There are 3 ways to pass on the data communication.

CRSP will access your server through VPN/RDP/Go to your PC where you will provide the user ID and password. We will access the client data to work remotely. Final processed data will be stored in your server.

Second way is to scan the data at your office and email it to CRSP.In turn, CRSP will process the data and store it on the server. The client will have 24-hour access to their the data from our server.

Third way is to scan the data and upload it on the cloud server. We will access and process the data from cloud and store a backup on the cloud for your access. There will be a user name and password provided to CRSP.
